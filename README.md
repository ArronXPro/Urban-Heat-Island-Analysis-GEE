# Urban Heat Island Analysis Using Google Earth Engine

## Overview

This project demonstrates the analysis of the **Urban Heat Island (UHI)** effect using **Google Earth Engine (GEE)** and satellite imagery. The objective is to identify areas with higher land surface temperatures, compare thermal patterns across the study area, and visualize urban heat distribution through thematic maps.

The project was completed as part of my B.Tech Geoinformatics coursework at Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad (JNTUH).

---

## Objectives

* Analyze urban heat distribution using satellite imagery.
* Generate Land Surface Temperature (LST) maps.
* Identify Urban Heat Island hotspots.
* Visualize temperature variations across the study area.
* Demonstrate the use of Google Earth Engine for geospatial analysis.

---

## Study Area

The analysis was conducted on a selected urban region for educational purposes.

## Methodology

1. Selected the study area.
2. Retrieved Landsat satellite imagery from Google Earth Engine.
3. Applied preprocessing and cloud filtering where applicable.
4. Calculated Land Surface Temperature (LST).
5. Visualized temperature distribution using thematic color palettes.
6. Identified Urban Heat Island hotspots.
7. Exported the final maps for analysis and reporting.
